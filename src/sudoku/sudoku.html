SUDOKU SOLVER



If a square is clicked on, that square becomes highlighted.
Typing a number on a highlighted square enters that number
into the square. The space bar clears a square.

The SOLVE button attempts to solve the puzzle.
The ERASE button clears out all numbers in the puzzle.
The CLEAN button clears out data not entered by the user.
The HELP button toggles this dialog (CLOSE clears).
